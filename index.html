Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rescue Bridge?

+

Rescue Bridge is a platform dedicated to connecting individuals and businesses with surplus items to those in need. Whether it's leftover food from events, unused clothing, or groceries nearing their expiration date, Rescue Bridge ensures these items are donated to people who can use them. Our mission is to reduce waste and provide support to communities through efficient and compassionate resource redistribution.

How Rescue Bridge Works?

+

Rescue Bridge works by allowing donors to register and submit their surplus items, which are then reviewed and collected by our team for distribution to local charities, shelters, and food banks, ensuring that resources reach those in need and reducing waste.

Goal of Rescue Bridge

+

The goal of Rescue Bridge is to efficiently redistribute surplus resources to those in need, reducing waste and fostering a more sustainable and compassionate community. By connecting donors with beneficiaries, we aim to address pressing social issues while promoting a sense of shared responsibility and empowerment among individuals and businesses alike.
